The Old Fashioned

Overview

The Old Fashioned is one of the most venerable of cocktails, predating not only the motorcar but the presidency of Abe Lincoln.

All it takes is serving whiskey, sugar, bitters and, if you like, a cherry or orange garnish on the rocks.

Properly made, it's strong but not too strong, sweet but not too sweet, and, most importantly, it's dead-simple and absolutely delicious.

Shopping List

  • 2 ounces bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 2 bashes bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • Garnish: 1 Luxardo cherry

Recipe

  1. Combine whiskey, bitters, and sugar into a mixing glass
  2. Add several large ice cubes and stir with a bar spoon
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with fresh ice
  4. Garnish: Add cherry to the cocktail
